## High Risk Ratios
The term "high risk" means this subset of the population has an increased risk of hospitalization and death when infected with influenza. Therefore is best used with the SEIHRD compartmental model that has hospitalization (H) and death (D) compartments to capture this increase. It is however required file for all models and can impact intervention strategies, such as vaccination only available for 65+ and high risk population.
